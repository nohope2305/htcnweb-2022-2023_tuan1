A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the mouth. It is
believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes,
an action known as a "Duchenne simle". Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
